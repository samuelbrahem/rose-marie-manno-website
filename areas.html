Deep Dive Into Each Community

Get to know the character, lifestyle, and market outlook for every area Rose Marie serves.

White Rock

Perched along the Semiahmoo Bay shoreline, White Rock is one of Metro Vancouver's most cherished coastal communities. Famous for its iconic 1,500-foot pier, the vibrant Marine Drive restaurant scene, and some of the mildest year-round temperatures in all of Canada, it draws everyone from retirees seeking a relaxed seaside lifestyle to young professionals and families craving ocean-view living without sacrificing urban convenience.

Avg. Home Price ~$1.6M
Lifestyle Coastal & Relaxed
Key Attractions White Rock Pier, Beach Promenade, Marine Drive Dining

South Surrey

South Surrey is synonymous with prestige and family living. Spanning established enclaves such as Morgan Creek, Grandview Heights, Ocean Park, and Crescent Beach, the area consistently ranks among the region's most desirable addresses. Top-rated public and private schools, beautifully maintained parks, and seamless access to both the U.S. border and downtown Vancouver make it an ideal choice for families and professionals who want space without sacrificing connectivity.

Avg. Home Price ~$1.4M
Lifestyle Prestigious & Family-Oriented
Key Attractions Morgan Creek, Crescent Beach, Ocean Park, Top Schools

Langley

Langley is one of the Fraser Valley's fastest-growing communities, seamlessly blending urban sophistication with pastoral charm. From the master-planned neighbourhoods of Willoughby Heights and Walnut Grove to the heritage allure of Fort Langley wine country, Langley offers remarkable lifestyle diversity. With the upcoming SkyTrain extension set to transform connectivity, the area represents one of the region's strongest investment corridors for the decade ahead.

Avg. Home Price ~$1.1M
Lifestyle Urban & Pastoral Blend
Key Attractions Fort Langley, Willoughby Heights, SkyTrain Extension

Surrey

As British Columbia's second-largest and fastest-growing city, Surrey is undergoing a remarkable transformation fuelled by billions of dollars in infrastructure investment and transit expansion. From the established charm of Cloverdale and South Surrey to the urban energy of Guildford and City Centre, the city's diverse neighbourhoods cater to every lifestyle and budget. Savvy buyers and investors are recognizing Surrey's exceptional long-term growth potential.

Avg. Home Price ~$980K
Lifestyle Diverse & Dynamic
Key Attractions Guildford, Cloverdale, Transit Expansion, Cultural Diversity

Port Coquitlam

Nestled along the Pitt and Coquitlam rivers, Port Coquitlam offers a genuinely welcoming, family-oriented community with impressive recreational amenities. Sought-after neighbourhoods like Burke Mountain and Citadel Heights combine mountain-backed scenery with modern homes and excellent schools. Outstanding transit options, including the West Coast Express, connect residents to downtown Vancouver in under 30 minutes, while the Traboulay PoCo Trail and numerous parks keep outdoor enthusiasts engaged year-round.

Avg. Home Price ~$950K
Lifestyle Family & Nature-Focused
Key Attractions Burke Mountain, West Coast Express, Traboulay Trail

Greater Vancouver

Greater Vancouver is consistently ranked among the world's most liveable and desirable metropolitan regions. From sleek downtown glass towers and trendy Yaletown lofts to the serene mountain retreats of the North Shore and the heritage neighbourhoods of East Vancouver, the metro offers extraordinary range. Home prices span from approximately $600K condos to $10M-plus waterfront estates, and Rose Marie's comprehensive regional knowledge ensures you find the right fit regardless of where your search leads.

Avg. Home Price $600K – $10M+
Lifestyle World-Class & Varied
Key Attractions Stanley Park, Granville Island, North Shore Mountains, Cultural Diversity
